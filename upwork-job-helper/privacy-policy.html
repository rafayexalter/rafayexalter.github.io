Privacy Policy for Upwork Job Helper

Effective date:

Upwork Job Helper is a Chrome extension designed to help users filter job posts on Upwork.com by country, keyword, and payment verification status. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or sensitive user data. All filter settings (such as your chosen countries, keywords, and payment preferences) are stored locally in your browser and are never shared with any third party or external server.

How the Extension Works

  • The extension only interacts with Upwork.com to apply your chosen filters to job posts.
  • It does not access, collect, or modify data on any other websites.
  • No data is transmitted outside your device.

Third-Party Services

Upwork Job Helper does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy while using this extension, please contact the developer at contact@abdulrafay.me.
